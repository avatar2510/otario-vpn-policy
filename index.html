Privacy Policy for OtarioVPN

Last updated: December 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to OtarioVPN ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our VPN application.

2. Information We Collect

We strive for minimal data collection necessary to provide our services:

Authentication Data

  • Email addresses for account creation
  • Firebase authentication tokens

User Account Data

  • User unique identifier (UID)
  • Email address
  • Premium subscription status
  • Administrator status (for service accounts)
  • Account creation date

Device Data

  • Device identifier
  • Device name
  • Platform (iOS/Android)
  • Last login time
  • Device record creation date

Free User Usage Data

  • IP address for tracking free usage limits (30 minutes)
  • Remaining usage time in seconds

Subscription Data

  • Active subscription status via Adapty
  • Payment information (processed by Google Play/App Store)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information as follows:

  • To provide VPN services
  • To authenticate users
  • To manage devices (maximum 3 devices per account)
  • To enforce free usage limits
  • To process payments and manage subscriptions
  • To improve app performance
  • To comply with legal requirements

4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties without your consent, except as described in this policy:

  • With trusted service providers who assist us in operating our service (Firebase, Adapty)
  • When required by law or to protect our rights
  • In connection with a business transfer or acquisition

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

6. Logging Policy

OtarioVPN strives for minimal data collection:

We DO NOT store:

  • User real IP addresses (except for tracking free user limits)
  • Browsing history or DNS queries
  • Detailed bandwidth statistics per user
  • Full connection logs

We only store:

  • Necessary data for service operation (accounts, devices)
  • Aggregated data for service improvement

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: Request access to your personal data
  • Rectification: Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data
  • Portability: Request transfer of your data
  • Objection: Object to processing of your data

8. Cookies and Tracking

We may use essential cookies for basic app functionality. We do not use tracking cookies for advertising purposes. You can control cookie preferences through your browser settings.

9. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 18.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
