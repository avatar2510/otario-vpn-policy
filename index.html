Privacy Policy for OtarioVPN

Last updated: December 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to OtarioVPN ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our VPN service.

2. Information We Collect (No-Logs Policy)

We respect your privacy and enforce a strict no-logs policy. To provide our service, we only collect the absolute minimum information required:

  • Account Information: Your email address and account credentials used for signing up and logging in.
  • Payment Information: When you subscribe, our third-party payment processors (like Google Play or Apple App Store) collect payment information. We do not store your full credit card details.
  • Aggregated & Anonymized Data: We may collect anonymous, aggregated data for service improvement, such as total bandwidth usage or server load statistics. This data cannot be tied back to any specific user.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the minimal information we collect in the following ways:

  • To provide, maintain, and operate our VPN service.
  • To process payments and manage your subscription.
  • To communicate with you regarding your account or our service (e.g., for customer support).
  • To improve and optimize our service performance and features.
  • To comply with legal obligations.

4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties without your consent, except as described in this policy:

  • With trusted service providers who assist us in operating our service (e.g., payment processors), under strict confidentiality agreements.
  • When required by law or to protect our rights and the safety of others.
  • In connection with a business transfer, merger, or acquisition.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

6. Strict No-Logs Policy

OtarioVPN is a strict no-logs VPN service. This means we **DO NOT** store or track:

  • Your real IP address or the VPN IP address assigned to you.
  • Your browsing history or DNS queries.
  • Connection timestamps, session duration, or bandwidth usage details per user.

We only retain minimal account information necessary for service operation, as stated in Section 2.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: Request access to the personal data we hold about you.
  • Rectification: Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Deletion: Request the deletion of your account and associated personal data.
  • Objection: Object to the processing of your data in certain circumstances.

8. Cookies and Tracking

We may use essential cookies on our website for basic functionality, such as keeping you logged in. We do not use tracking cookies for advertising purposes. You can control cookie preferences through your browser settings.

9. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 18.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
